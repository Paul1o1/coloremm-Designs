DOCUMENTARY FILM PRODUCTION AND PHOTOGRAPHY

PINK GIRL (POWER IN KNOWLEDGE) IS AN ORGANIZATION THAT ORIGINATED FROM A DREAM TO IMPROVE THE LIVES OF GIRLS IN NORTHEASTERN ETHIOPIA. IT FOCUSES ON PROVIDING TUTORING AND LIFE SKILLS PROGRAMS TO EMPOWER ETHIOPIAN GIRLS . COLOREM IS HAPPY TO HAVE BEEN ABLE TO TELL PINK GIRL'S STORY. WE TOOK PHOTOS AND MADE VIDEOS TO SHOW HOW THEY'RE HELPING THESE GIRLS TO LEARN MORE AND DO WELL IN SCHOOL. OUR WORK HELPED HIGHLIGHT HOW THESE GIRLS ARE SUCCEEDING AND GOING TO COLLEGE, THANKS TO PINK GIRL. WE LOVE USING OUR SKILLS TO SUPPORT GOOD CAUSES AND MAKE A DIFFERENCE.
